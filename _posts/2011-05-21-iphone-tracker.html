I did a bit more research and found an application developed by some guy that would plot the recorded locations on a map. When I inputted my iPhone backup files to program, I was surprised by the results. The application did work as expected, but apparently the location logging and my backup files extended back to when I did my cross-country. That said, there are positions logged all the way across the United States showing where my Uncle and I landed and stayed for the night. Click on the image to see more!
